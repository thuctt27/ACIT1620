Biography

Thuc is a highly accomplished and versatile individual with an impressive background in culinary arts, aviation, lifesaving, and literature.
Born in Ho Chi Minh City, Thuc has pursued excellence in every endeavor.
As a chef, they trained under the renowned Gordon Ramsay and honed their craft in four Michelin-starred restaurants, creating unforgettable dining experiences.

Beyond these fields, Thuc has demonstrated heroism as a lifeguard, saving three lives and showcasing their quick thinking and compassion.
As an author, Thuc’s creativity shines through in three New York Times bestsellers, captivating readers worldwide with engaging and impactful stories.
Whether in the kitchen, the cockpit, on the beach, or behind a desk, Thuc’s diverse achievements reflect their passion for excellence, relentless curiosity, and drive to make a meaningful impact in every facet of life.
